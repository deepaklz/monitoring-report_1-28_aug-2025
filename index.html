6. Insights / Highlights

6.1 Overall Key Trends: Customer base grew by +6 paid accounts; Maharashtra (Kolhapur cluster) continues as the dominant acquisition zone. Software ON remains strong (110/120 active), with “WhatsApp Suggestion Reading” as the most popular feature (used by 86 customers). Software churn risk: 5 customers turned off features this period — Alchemy Precision Cast, S.N.S. Alloy, and Top-Gear are most critical to re-engage.

6.2 Module Specific - ChargeMix: Strong adoption in Brakes India divisions (39 and 8 modifications vs. previous 13 and 2). Crescent Foundry dropped sharply from 31 to 4. Needs investigation.

6.3 Module Specific - Heat Plan: Vishwakarma Founders is a power-user (75/day vs benchmark 45). Belgaum FerroCast & Crescent running below benchmarks (43 vs 68, 9.5 vs 40). NIPHA has completely stopped — possible disengagement risk.

7. Recommendations for Product & Ops

  • Target Kolhapur cluster further → continues to be a major adoption zone.
  • Intervene with Crescent & NIPHA (low activity, stopped heat plan, dropped HMT).
  • Create a parsing error taskforce: focus Sapre, Parvati, Nipha duplicate files.
  • Engage customers who turned software off (Alchemy, Top-Gear, S.N.S.).
  • Hardware push: Improve engagement for tablets; leverage Spectro TV as proven sticky.
