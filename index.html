Insight: Steady Customer Growth

The report highlights a steady increase in the paid customer base, with **6 new customers** onboarded this period. The majority of these new customers have opted for the "Only Reading" feature, which suggests that a low-commitment, data-focused entry point is a successful acquisition strategy.

Insight: Mixed Engagement & Retention

While customer acquisition is positive, the report also flags potential retention issues. Several customers have turned off their software, and some, like NIPHA EXPORTS, are experiencing a high parsing failure rate of **1.11%** in their Microstructure reports, indicating a potential technical issue that needs to be addressed.

Insight: High-Performing Customers

On a positive note, a few customers, such as **Vishwakarma Founders** and **Mal Metalliks**, are showing exceptional engagement with heat plan creation. They are significantly exceeding their benchmark averages, which is a strong indicator of the software's value and successful adoption in these specific cases.

Insight: Hardware Usage Disparity

The data shows a clear disparity in hardware usage, with **Spectro TV** being the most active device. This suggests an opportunity to promote the usage of other devices like the Spectro-Tablet and Heat Plan-Tablet, which are currently seeing lower engagement, to unlock their full potential and provide more value to customers.
